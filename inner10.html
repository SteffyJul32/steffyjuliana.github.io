Designing a mobile app for ticket sales

Creating UI UX Design for a mobile app to sell, buy and store ticket

Background

I was given a task to design UI and UX for a ticket selling app that is meant to be a wallet and a marketplace. So in the app, customers can view purchased ticket from the website. At the same time, they also can sell and buy ticket using the app. The app will be a marketplace for resale concert tickets. Those who purchased a ticket but ended up not going can sell through this app. Transaction through the app will be highly secured.

So the process that I did here was:

  • To have a discussion with my boss who was the business owner to understand what outcomes that expect from the app.
  • Did a competitor analysis to see how other companies do the business.
  • The market situation of reselling in Singapore.

To kickstart, we did a user research to understand the journey

To kickstart the design process, we started by designing wireframe to determine the UX.

After approval, then we started designing the UI.

Design system

Typography and colour

Component
We design the basic components that are going to be used across the app.

The process of adding components to design system will go together with designing the UI.

In designing the UI, we also keep in mind that:

  • We want the elements to affect customers' emotion and therefore, we apply different label and animation that can trigger scarcity to speed up purchase decision making.
  • We want the UI to be modern, fun and intuitive.
  • We use human's face as main design for interaction to build deep engagement with customers. (source: https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/psychology/articles/10.3389/fpsyg.2015.00909/full)

Expected outcomes is people will be able to use the app for ticket sales and digital ticket wallet.
