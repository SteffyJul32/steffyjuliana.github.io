Designing a website for ticket sales

Creating UI UX Design for online platforms to sell ticket

Background

I was approached by client to provide UI UX service and consultancy for his startup project which is a ticket selling company. The project is meant to launch in January 2025. It is a website and a mobile app to help customers purchase concert tickets. Take Carousell as a reference for this project but focusing on concert ticket. The unique selling point for this project will be its built-in security through out the entire end to end buying and selling journey.


Website ideation and design

Product Roadmap
To start with the project, I created a product roadmap after rounds of discussion with the client which will be remain confidential. I always use Outcome-Based Roadmaps to identify features that needs to be built in the website.

Design thinking

For design thinking, I am using Double Diamond design thinking which is commonly used to design UX for a digital product.

Discovery

The discovery method that I used was Competitor Analysis, Market Research and User Interview.

I went through different competitors' websites to understand their business and platforms' UX. The client had also passed me a market research to help me understand the market situation, target audience and expected goals. The user interview that I did was with my relatives who had used some of the websites to purchase tickets.

Define

After the discovery phase, I managed to understand the journey and audience who'll use the website. So I created a customer journey and sitemap to help me empathising with how users interact with the platform usually.
