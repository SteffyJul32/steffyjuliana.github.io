Designing a website for ticket sales

Creating UI UX Design for online platforms to sell ticket

Background

This is a documentation I made to design a buying journey UX for a startup website. The website is planned to sell resale concert tickets. And I was tasked to design the buying journey. A journey for customers to purchase tickets. Before starting to design, I started by doing a research to empathize with both business and customers. Luckily, there are many competitors to learn from that gives references as a foundation.

Empathize

User interviews
As a first step, I started by interviewing my friends who had purchased from resale ticket platforms. My questions for them were meant to understand:
• Their initial intention • Their journey of finding the platforms
• The journey in using the platforms • Their satisfaction after using the platforms
• Their suggestion on the platforms' improvement if any

Competitor analysis
Comparing different competitor's platforms and journeys.

Define

Customer journey
After interviews, it gave me ideas of what the users went through when they were about to purchase tickets. It also allows me to create a customer journey as a way to map the journey.

Based on the journey, I proceed with blueprint service mapping to figure out the screen journey. This mapping helps me to figure out all features that need to be added on the buying journey.

Since there will be more tickets, we are going to work with OCR feature where we are displaying the text from tickets automatically using Amazon Textract. Through ticket analyse, I studied similarity of all tickets to be used as a filter that will help users to

Filter for ticket will be based on:
• Ticket type
• Category
• Section
• Price
